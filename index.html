This cat right here

is Fru

He may look small and relatively unharmful, but the reality is that
he is quite dirty and evil and mischevous and tricksy
when he wants to be.

Some Fru Facts

He enjoys sleeping near near others during the day.

Loud sounds always seem to get his attention no matter where he is.

He loves to take refuge under blankets, bags, or other forms of cover

It may not always seem like it, but he loves people, and longs for attention

“If animals could speak, the dog would be a blundering outspoken fellow; but the cat would have the rare grace of never saying a word too much. I love cats because I enjoy my home; and little by little, they become its visible soul.”

-Fru, Cat of Catastrophe
